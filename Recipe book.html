Pasta salad with tuna, capers & balsamic dressing

  • Time: 10 minutes preparing and 10 minutes cooking.
  • Serves: 4
Ingredients Quantity
orecchiette pasta 350g
jar MSC approved tuna in spring water, drained 225g
caper, drained 1 tbsp
peppers from a jar, chopped 15 peppadew
celery heart, sliced 350g
yellow, red or a mixture of cherry tomato, halved 140g
balsamic vinegar/td> 75ml
extra-virgin olive oil 3 tbsp
bag rocket leaves 100g
good handful basil leaves ---

Step 1: Cook the pasta following pack instructions, then drain and rinse in cold water. After draining again, transfer to a large bowl. Add the remaining ingredients except the basil, season well, and toss to combine. Scatter with basil and serve.
